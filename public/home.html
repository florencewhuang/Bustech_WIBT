WOMEN IN BUSINESS TECHNOLOGY




About Us

Our Club

WIBT (Women in Business Technology) is a club at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. The club strives to increase female representation and break down barriers in the Business Technology industry, shaping the future environment for women entering the field. This is accomplished through different events held within the club including hosting speakers, networking events, professional development, DEI (diversity, equity, and inclusion), and community service.

Our Purpose

In creating this information systems database, having a resource where members can easily access events would allow for an easy retrieval of information to look back on.
